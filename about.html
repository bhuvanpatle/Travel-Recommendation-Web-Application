Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

TravelBloom was founded with a vision to revolutionize how people discover and plan their travels. Started as a small team of passionate travelers.

2021

First Milestone

Reached 10,000 users and launched our personalized recommendation engine, helping travelers find destinations that match their preferences.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded our destination database to cover over 100 countries and partnered with local tourism boards for authentic experiences.

2023

Innovation Award

Received the "Best Travel Tech Innovation" award and launched our mobile app with advanced AI-powered recommendations.

2024

Sustainable Travel Initiative

Launched our eco-friendly travel program, promoting sustainable tourism and carbon-neutral travel options.

2025

Looking Forward

Continuing to innovate with new features like virtual reality destination previews and AI-powered itinerary planning.
